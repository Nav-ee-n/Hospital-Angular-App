About Us

SK Hospital was established in the year 2003 and inaugurated by the then Chief Minister of Kerala Shri. A K Antony who later went on to become the Defence Minister of India. Started as a modest 30 bed in-patient facility along with few departments, SK Hospital has now become a tertiary care NABH Safe-I certified multi-speciality healthcare institution with 300 beds.

Vision

To achieve excellence in health care and service to all patients. Build a World-Class Health Care unit with professional expertise, streamlined efficacy in service and courteous personal attention in a meticulously clean environment at affordable levels.
